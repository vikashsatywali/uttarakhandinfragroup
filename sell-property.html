TRANSPARENCY

Common Questions From Buyers & Sellers

How do you calculate a property's value?

We don't rely purely on "market rumors". Our civil engineers evaluate the structural integrity, and our legal team assesses region-specific circle rates and demand metrics to formulate a verifiable, fair offer.

Do you purchase or sell disputed properties?

We are quite strict. We only deal in properties with 100% clean legal titles, cleared mutations, and verifiable chain-of-ownership documents to ensure entirely ethical transfers for buyers and sellers.

Will I need a broker?

Not at all. Uttarakhand Infra Groups buys and sells directly. Sidestepping third parties ensures 0% hidden brokerage fees reducing your overall costs.

How fast is the payout or registry?

Once legal vetting gives a green light, registry and the concurrent RTGS/NEFT bank transfers are executed simultaneously. Typical closures happen within 14-21 working days for both buying and selling.
