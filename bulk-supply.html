The Procurement Engine

How Bulk Operations Work

Requirement Profiling

  • Analyzing the exact BOQ (Bill of Quantities) of your massive project
  • Determining the material testing standards required (e.g., specific Bajri grade)
  • Calculating the most efficient logistical drop-zones on site

Direct Mill Sourcing

  • Bypassing local city retailers to negotiate directly with factory heads
  • Securing bulk material at base commodity line prices
  • Guaranteeing zero material adulteration right from the source

Dedicated Fleet Locking

  • Assigning massive 12-tyre and 14-tyre dumpers exclusively for your route
  • Reserving closed container fleets for weather-sensitive cement hauls
  • Ensuring round-the-clock uninterrupted shuttle operations

Weighbridge Audits

  • 120% strict compliance with computerized "Dharamkanta" (weighbridge) slips
  • Complete transparency on exact tonnage dispatched vs delivered
  • Absolute eradication of transit material theft or spillage

Massive Discharge

  • Coordinating multiple truck unloads simultaneously at your staging area
  • Mechanical tipping and hydraulic lifts for extremely fast turnaround times
  • Keeping your local workforce constantly fed with raw building material

Net Billing Cycle

  • Providing consolidated weekly or monthly transport-inclusive billing
  • Simplified GST invoicing merging the purchase and freight costs
  • Creating profound capital efficiency for your construction budget
