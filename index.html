Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What regions do you primarily serve for construction?

We primarily operate across Uttarakhand, focusing extensively on Dehradun, Mussoorie, Hardiwar, Rishikesh, and the surrounding hill stations. Our expertise lies in earthquake-resistant engineering suited for the Himalayan topography.

Is your logistics fleet equipped for heavy industrial transit?

Absolutely. Our fleet includes heavy-duty trailers, multi-axle trucks, and specialized carriers specifically modified to navigate steep and narrow mountain roads while carrying heavy machinery and industrial raw materials securely.

How do you handle corporate relocations?

We provide a turnkey relocation solution. This includes professional packing of IT equipment and office furniture, secure transit, and meticulous reassembly at the new location, ensuring minimal downtime for your business operations.

Do you offer warehousing facilities?

Yes, we have secure, 24/7 monitored transit warehousing facilities in Dehradun and Haridwar to accommodate both short-term logistics holding and long-term storage needs during complex relocation projects.

Are your construction projects environmentally compliant?

Environmental sustainability is core to our operations in Uttarakhand Infra Groups. All our projects strictly adhere to local environmental guidelines, utilizing sustainable materials where possible and implementing robust waste management protocols.
