Flawless Logistics

Moving a car or a motorcycle requires far more than just loading it onto a truck. We factor in suspension care, brake alignment, and weather variables to provide a pristine delivery experience.

GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does car transportation take?

Transit time depends on the destination. Local moving within Uttarakhand takes 1-2 days, while PAN India delivery generally spans 5-10 business days based on highway traffic and weather conditions.

Can I pack personal items inside the car?

For safety and regulatory reasons, we strongly advise against leaving valuables inside. However, up to 10-15 kg of personal, non-valuable items can be kept in the trunk. It is not insured against loss.

Do you provide door-to-door pickup?

Yes, our driver will come to your original address, inspect the vehicle, load it, and deliver it exactly at your new destination doorstep without you needing to visit any hub.

Are my vehicles insured during transit?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive transit insurance. Before the move, a detailed condition report is signed, making sure you are financially protected against any unforeseen damage.
