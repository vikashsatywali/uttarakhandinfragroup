GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my goods insured while in storage?

Yes, we offer comprehensive in-storage insurance options covering fire, theft, and natural disasters to ensure absolute financial protection while your goods reside with us.

Can I access my belongings at any time?

Access arrangements depend on your service plan. Standard storage requires a 24-48 hour notice for un-racking, while premium accessible units allow for easy regular self-access.

What items are restricted from being stored?

We strictly prohibit the storage of flammable liquids, hazardous chemicals, perishable foods, illegal substances, live animals, and highly sensitive undocumented firearms.

Do you pack the items before storage?

Absolutely. Our expert crew comes to your location to execute professional-grade packing, utilizing high-quality materials to guarantee goods are safely buffered for prolonged holding.
