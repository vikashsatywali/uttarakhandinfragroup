The Heritage

The Uttarakhand Infra Groups Evolution

2011

The localized genesis

Initiated as a specialized construction material supplier in Rudrapur, providing indispensable elements to independent civil contractors.

2015

Engineering Expansion

Acquisition of our first multi-axle fleet. Officially branched into full-scale residential and commercial Turnkey Construction execution.

2019

Executive Relocations

Established our highly successful Premium Movers & Packers division, recognizing the massive regional need for secure corporate shifting.

Today

A National Benchmark

Operating a vast ecosystem of infrastructure services, real estate consultation, and cross-state transport routes scaling All India.

Building For Tomorrow

As a massive consumer of fuel and natural building resources, we recognize our heavy ecological footprint. Here is how we are mitigating it.

BS-VI Fleet Compliance

Upgrading our entire logistics network to BS-VI engines to drastically cut road emissions during cross-state deliveries.

Material Recycling

Responsibly sorting and recycling steel scrap and concrete refuse from construction demolition sites.

Eco-Friendly Packing

Gradually phasing out toxic plastics in our Movers & Packers division for biodegradable, robust foam alternatives.
