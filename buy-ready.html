KNOWLEDGE HUB

Frequently Asked Questions

Are colony plots approved by authorities?

Absolutely. Every gated society and colony we represent has been vetted and approved by specific regional development authorities like MDDA, SIDA, or local Nagar Nigams depending on the zone.

Do you help with interior modifications?

Yes. As a primary construction and development firm, Uttarakhand Infra Groups can alter modular kitchens, flooring, or color palettes of "Ready-to-Move" houses before you transition in.

Is a ready-to-move costlier than under-construction?

While base costs might seem slightly higher, you save heavily on waiting time, rent, and inflation. Plus, there is zero risk of project stall, making ready-to-move financially brilliant.

What are the maintenance charges in societies?

It varies based on luxury segments. Standard colonies range from ₹1000 - ₹2500 per month governing park maintenance, security staff, and common streetlights.
