Power Your Infrastructure

Purchasing heavy machinery is often financially inviable for individual projects. Maintaining and storing massive machines eats heavily into your profit margins and operational time.

Uttarakhand Infra Groups Logistics offers an on-demand, highly specialized heavy machinery rental service. With expert certified operators provided, we bring sheer mechanical power exactly where and when you need it.

From massive earthmoving for deep mountain foundations to high-reach cranes lifting pre-cast columns, our perfectly serviced equipment guarantees zero breakdown delays.

Earthmovers & JCBs

Heavy-duty Backhoe Loaders and powerful excavators for quick trenching, massive levelling, road clearing, and deep foundation digging.

Hydraulic Dumpers

Massive payload tipper trucks capable of hauling thousands of tons of gravel, excavated soil, and demolition debris rapidly away from your site.

Mobile Cranes

High-reach hydraulic telescopic cranes for securely lifting extremely heavy steel beams, industrial machinery, and pre-cast concrete blocks.

Transit Mixers (RMC)

Rotary drum transit mixers ensuring continuous, uninterrupted pouring of high-grade ready-mix concrete for massive commercial floor slabs.

Road Rollers & Compactors

Vibratory soil compactors and tandem rollers essential for achieving perfect ground density before pouring foundations or laying asphalt roads.
