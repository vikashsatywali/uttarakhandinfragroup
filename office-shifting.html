ELIMINATING DOWNTIME

Because Time is Money

Relocating an office is vastly different from moving a house. In business, every hour your team cannot work translates directly to lost revenue. Our core objective is absolute speed mixed with maximum corporate security.

Whether you're shifting a 20-employee startup across Dehradun or moving a 500-workstation enterprise out of SIDCUL Pantnagar, Uttarakhand Infra Groups plans weekend shifts that guarantee your teams are back online by Monday morning.

Absolute IT Security. We specialize in the safe extraction, anti-static packing, and precise re-installation of Server Racks, Data Centers, and Sensitive Electronics.

IT & Server Relocation

Using specialized anti-static bubbled wraps and shock-proof crates to safely transfer highly sensitive server racks and network switches.

Workstation Disassembly

Expert carpenters securely dismantle complex modular cubicles, manager desks, and boardroom assemblies, mapping each piece for rebuild.

Secure Document Archiving

Label-mapped, lockable rigid crates ensuring critical HR documents, legal filings, and financial records remain highly secure and organized.

Heavy Safes & Lockers

Deployment of specialized hoisting equipment, heavy-duty trolleys, and hydraulic tail-lifts for shifting massive fireproof safes.

Weekend "Zero-Downtime" Shifts

Our crews operate through Friday nights and weekends, ensuring your entire organizational setup is plug-and-play ready by Monday.

E-Waste & Asset Disposal

Optional liquidation or ethical disposal of outdated, unneeded office furniture, old computers, and broken chairs during the move.

The Corporate Methodology

How Your Business Moves

Blueprint Mapping

  • On-site survey by an enterprise move-manager
  • Mapping the new office floorplan to exact employee desks
  • Creating a strict departmental moving sequence (HR, IT, Sales)

Logical Disconnect

  • Coordinating directly with your internal IT department
  • Systematic shutdown and precise un-cabling of all hardware
  • Color-coded tagging for every monitor, keyboard, and CPU

Commercial Packing

  • Delivering pre-move crates for employees to pack their desk items
  • Executing heavy industrial wrapping of all cubicles and chairs
  • Securing glass partitions and delicate projector equipment

Fleet Transfer

  • Loading into GPS-tracked, secure containerized trucks
  • Maintaining continuous security supervision during transit
  • Ensuring absolute adherence to building management rules

Floor Rebuild

  • Unloading directly to the corresponding floors and zones
  • Rapid reassembly of all workstations and conference tables
  • Assisting IT teams in re-racking the primary server clusters

Operations Go-Live

  • Final quality check of all fixtures and removed debris
  • Handover to the Operations Manager for sign-off
  • Employees walk in to a fully functional space on day one

GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you move servers and IT equipment?

Yes, we use anti-static padding and shock-proof crates to safely relocate entire data centers, sensitive server racks, switches, and high-end IT systems.

Can business operations continue during the move?

We typically execute office shifts during weekends or extended holidays to ensure a 'Zero Downtime' transition. Your employees can leave Friday and resume work on Monday at the new location.

Are confidential files handled securely?

Absolutely. We provide tamper-proof, rigid and label-mapped crates specifically for sensitive HR files, financial documents, and proprietary assets.

Can you help dismantle modular cubicles?

Yes, our in-house expert carpenters map, dismantle, and fully reassemble workstations, boardroom tables, and complex office cubicles at the destination.
